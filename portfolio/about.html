About

Hi, my name is Jacob Burnstein. I am a Software Developer who builds tools and systems that help people. I am also a professional musician, amateur home cook, and all-around hobbyist.

Before my shift into web development, I was VP of Day Services at a music-infused program that helps adults with intellectual disabilities build social, employment, and independence skills.

Through strong leadership, analytical problem-solving, and adaptability, I provided resources and created sustainable systems to ensure my 70+ clients and 30+ staff had tools for success.

My creative affinity for building efficient tools drew me to writing code. Because I love working in methodical and incremental steps to achieve a greater goal, everything clicked when a close friend started teaching me the basics.

Only strengthened by my experience completing Fullstack Academy's web development bootcamp, I found assets in my transferrable skills that helped me to build my strong foundations in end-to-end software development.

I am excited by technologies that make a positive impact on the world, and collaborating with other developers who share those values.
