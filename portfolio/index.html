Creator & Developer

CheckList Assist

This project was not only a means to challenge myself with unfamiliar technologies but was also just an app I needed.

My wife and I used to use a shared Apple notes file which was unreliably up-to-date, didn't provide a system to organize lists by store, and lacked a UI built for in-store shopping.

I built this app to be user-friendly and intuitive, because lists are lists, and don't need to be complicated.

Creator & Developer

Counter Amounter

Assistive technology helping individuals with intellectual disabilities build employment skills. Developed in collaboration with a team of 3 peers at Fullstack Academy.

Executing all functions of a typical cash register, the Counter Amounter also breaks down change for the user, letting them incrementally add money received, and shows a step-by-step breakdown of change to return to the customer.

Creator & Developer

(Out of Coffee) Ratio Calculator

Have you ever gone to make your morning coffee to then realize you don't have enough beans for your typical brew? How many grams do you have? Great, but how many cups of water should you pour in? It's 6am - too early for math.

This is an application that solves this simple yet troubling problem of insufficient coffee for a full pot. I created this as a useful tool for my daily life and it has proven valuable for cooking, beer-making and of course, coffee!

About Me

Before my shift into web development, I was VP of Day Services at a music-infused program that helps adults with intellectual disabilities build social, employment, and independence skills.

Through strong leadership, analytical problem-solving, and adaptability, I provided resources and created sustainable systems to ensure my 70+ clients and 30+ staff had tools for success.

Now I build web apps! My creative affinity for building tools drew me to writing code. Because I love working in methodical and incremental steps to achieve a greater goal, everything clicked when a close friend started teaching me the basics.
